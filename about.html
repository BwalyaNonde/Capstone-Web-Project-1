Girls in ICT 2022

The Girls in ICT summit brings together the community of experts, academics and activities who comprise the creative affliate network in a different country every year. This year, we hope to expand our invitation by including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the commons, free culture and open knowledge.

So if you are active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge - free software advocates, Wikipedians open knowledge, gallaries, Libraries, museums archives governments and foundations, lawyers and activists - we hope you will consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant commons together.
